Krug-Munroe Wedding

TBD Medford, OR

UPDATE: 11-May-2020

Dear Guests, Friends, and Family,

It is our sincerest regret to inform you that we have been forced to postpone our wedding due to COVID-19. Presently we are targeting a new date and believe that this is likely best positioned sometime in 2021 given the present state of the world.

We fully understand that counties in Oregon are beginning to re-open and a go-live for the wedding could potentially be “OK” by the time our original date comes around would be realistic. However, we are faced with the harsh reality of a supply chain problem. Many of our vendors in our supply chain for rentals and catering are no longer in business or have been forced to close up shop due to COVID-19.

Looks for more updates coming from us around Fall of 2020 with regard to the new date for our wedding. We’re sincerely looking forward to having this celebration with all of you in the traditional style. Please continue to look for updates and stay pragmatic as we navigate this difficult time.

Sincerely,

Andrew Krug & Jackie Munroe
