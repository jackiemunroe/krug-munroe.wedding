Places to Stay

Option 1 - Adventurer Package

Camp with us in our field on our 6 acre ranch. Don't worry we have a guest house with a nice bathroom and dedicated hot water you'll be able to share with others to grab a quick shower and use a toilet with real running water. See our FAQ for more camping Q&A.

Option 2 - Business Class Hotel

Stay at the Fabulous Homewood Suites with our wonderful friend and General Manager “Wendy”. Click Here to Reserve a Room

This hotel is just a short stay from our place just over the freeway. Probably about 11.00 to Lyft or Uber as well. Note: not particularly walkable but you could bike from there.

Option 3 - Luxury AirBnB or Ashland Hotel

One of our family members runs a number of AirBnB spaces in the area in his mansion and a historic road motel in Ashland. This is a bit furhter away but you'll enjoy amenities and scenic views of the Rogue Valley.

For AirBnB options call 541-613-7625
